## Track 3 — Enterprise Impact (`#g4hackathon-enterprise-impact`)

**Project Name:** Real-Time Deal Room

**Team Members:** @yourhandle

**Project Description:** Enterprises leak enormous value in live negotiations —
discounting too early, accepting bad contract redlines, and missing the buyer's
real objection. Real-Time Deal Room is a secure negotiation sidecar that fuses
CRM, pricing policy, security signals, and contract redlines (via Gemma 4 31B
multimodal vision) into one private, margin-aware recommendation delivered inside
the reply window. The API key stays server-side, outputs are schema-constrained
for safe rendering, and the system degrades to deterministic guidance on
failure — and at Cerebras speed a 1–3% lift in negotiation outcomes across
renewals and procurement is worth far more than another post-call summary tool.

**GitHub Repository:** https://github.com/yourname/real-time-deal-room
